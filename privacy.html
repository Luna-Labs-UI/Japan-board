Privacy Policy

How Japan Direct Jobs handles information when you visit our site. Short version: we collect almost nothing, and we store nothing about you.

Last updated: April 29, 2026
Plain English Summary

We do not create accounts, store personal data, or share your information with third parties. Clicking "Apply" takes you directly to the employer's website — we play no part in that transaction.

1. Who we are

Japan Direct Jobs is a job listing aggregator focused on direct-hire roles in Japan for ALTs, foreign nationals, and English-speaking professionals. The platform is operated independently and is not affiliated with any recruitment agency, staffing company, or the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare (MHLW).

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@japandirectjobs.com

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

We do not ask you to create an account, submit a CV, or fill in any forms. There are no login screens, no candidate profiles, and no application forms on this website.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting provider may collect standard server log data, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymised where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time of visit
  • Referring URL

This data is used solely for security monitoring and aggregate traffic analysis. It is not sold, rented, or shared with advertisers.

Cookies

We use a single functional cookie — jdj-theme — stored in localStorage to remember your light/dark mode preference. This is not a tracking cookie and contains no personal information.

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking scripts.

3. How we use information

  • To display job listings sourced from publicly available employer websites
  • To maintain site security and diagnose technical issues
  • To improve the site based on aggregate, anonymised usage patterns

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

4. Third-party links

Every job listing on Japan Direct Jobs links directly to the employer's official website or official government job portal. Once you click "Apply", you leave our site entirely. The destination website has its own privacy policy, which governs any data you submit there. We encourage you to read it before applying.

Important

We have no access to, and take no responsibility for, any data you submit to third-party employer or government websites after leaving Japan Direct Jobs.

5. Data retention

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete. Server log data (IP addresses, page views) is automatically purged by our hosting provider within 30 days.

6. Your rights under APPI

Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI) gives individuals rights over their personal data. Since we do not hold personal data about individual users, most APPI requests will not be applicable. However, if you believe we hold any information about you, you may contact us to request:

  • Disclosure of what data (if any) we hold
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of data
  • Suspension of use

We will respond to all requests within 14 business days.

7. Children's privacy

Japan Direct Jobs is intended for adults seeking employment. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our site, contact us immediately.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
